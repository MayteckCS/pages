Our Mission

MCSPages exists to strip away financial barriers. By leveraging low-overhead, high-performance technology like GitHub and Cloudflare, we provide world-class foundations for minority-owned startups.

Our goal is to jumpstart the "little guy" with our technical expertise, so they can focus on what they know best: running their business.

Minority Foundations

Direct support for underrepresented founders to secure their professional digital identity without monthly overhead.

Silver Tech

Specialized onboarding for community elders. We translate complex tech into simple, manageable business tools.

Zero-Cost Stack

Utilizing .pages.dev and GitHub for high-speed, secure, and permanent hosting at no cost to the founder.

"We believe digital equity is achieved when status no longer dictates the quality of a business's online presence."
